About

Technology has no intrinsic value. Value is created when it enriches people's lives. It's been my passion for more than 19 years to build software that does just that. I've worked across the spectrum — freelancer, agency co-founder, consultant, CTO, and senior IC — mostly in Ruby on Rails. Currently at Teamtailor in Gothenburg; previously three years at 37signals, shipping cycle projects on Basecamp and HEY.

What I care about most is the point where product thinking meets code quality: understanding what users actually need, scoping the work pragmatically, and translating it into code that's clean, tested, and maintainable. The shipped code isn't the point — what matters is whether what was built actually made someone's day better.
